Log In to GitHub Click on Your Avatar in the Upper Right Corner and Then “Your Profile” Click on “Repositories” in the Menu to the Right of Your Profile Image Scroll Repositories or Use the Search Bar and Click the Repository in Question Click “Settings” in the Menu Directly Below the Repository Name Scroll Down to the “Danger Zone” and click “Delete this repository” Type the Name of the Repository into the Pop-Up Window that Appears and Click the “I understand the consequences, delete this repository” button
